Our Story

A Culinary Journey
Like No Other

At Soulful n Beyond, we believe that dining is not merely about sustenance — it is a ceremony of the senses. Nestled in the heart of Bidhannagar, Kolkata, our restaurant is a sanctuary where time slows and every course is a conversation between heritage and innovation.

Our culinary philosophy draws from the soul of Bengal's rich food traditions, elevated through modern technique and the finest premium ingredients. Each dish is a study in contrast — bold and delicate, familiar and revelatory. From our hand-selected spices to our curated wine pairings, every detail is orchestrated to transcend the ordinary.
